Monet-Inspired Map

Designed for GEOG 572, this map style draws inspiration from Claude Monet and the Impressionist art movement. I attempted to translate the paint techniques into a digital cartographic design. The basetile design emphasizes soft color gradients, brush-like patterns, and subtle border variations. The textures seen throughout the map are vectorized snashots of various paintings from Monet.

This project is also presented as an interactive ArcGIS StoryMap.
